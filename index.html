Welcome To Suffah

Suffah Model School was started by Sir Abdul Majeed Kiyani (DEO) in 2008. In the beginning, classes Kids to 4 were run, and at that time there were only 30 pupils, 6 members of staff, 2 ayas and 1 office boy. The aim was to produce a wide spectrum of subjects for Matric. The worthy Director (Sir Abdul Majeed Kiyani) is an educationist of repute and had dedicated his life to serve the cause of quality education, for the prosperity of future generations of Pakistan. The thought of dynamism and future actions must be translated into reality.
